Biography

I am a second-year Computer Science student with a strong interest in building software that is both useful and easy to use. My academic interests lie mainly in web development and human-computer interaction, though I also enjoy the logic and structure of algorithms and data analysis. I like courses that let me build something tangible by the end of the semester rather than just studying theory in isolation.

My career goal is to become a full-stack software developer, working on products that make everyday tasks simpler for the people who use them. In the longer term, I would like to contribute to open-source projects and eventually mentor younger students the way senior students have mentored me.

I would describe my strengths as curiosity, patience with difficult problems, and a habit of asking "why" until I actually understand something rather than just memorising it. Outside of academics, I try to stay active and keep learning new skills, because I believe growth rarely happens by standing still.
